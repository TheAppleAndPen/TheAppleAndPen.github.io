About Me

Prateek is an undergraduate studying in the line of commerce. He always had a knack for writing but never went serious with it. That was until some time ago.

He started writing as a hobby for he was always interested in creative writing. So he started his writing career with a poem and a novel.

"A place to share my skills" is what Prateek wants. And so the idea of creating a webiste popped up in his mind. Always getting top scores in language and literature, he honed his skills in all the subjects.

From the state of West Bengal, India, he always focuses on the important stuff and does not waste a single moment on unnecessary things. Since childhood he has always had a curiousity to know everything. If there's a book, he tried to read all the books. If there's a movie, he watched all the movies. If there's any talks on media platforms giving information on topics like science, he would watch all the talks.

In short, he witnessed many things. And being the most fond of english, he ultimately developed a desire of wanting to write.

Why Apple&Pen?

To answer that question, well I originally hated eating apples and even looking at them made me sick when I was a kid. More so i hated essay writing for my assignments, yet I loved studies nevertheless. I couldn't even eat a quarter of an apple at a time, I was so scared of it. Despite all that I wanted to one day at least eat half of it without a single problem. So one day when I was trying to write an essay for my annual school magazine I couldn't come up with any ideas, as I was really weak on it. And at the time when I was most confuesd and was about to give up, my mom brought an apple to my desk, and in my frustration I ate all of it without even realizing. Since that time I am used to eating apples. But what drove me to writing was the fact that on that night I finally came up with an essay topic and soon it even got published. Hence the name Apple & Pen.
