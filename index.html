About Me

Prateek is an undergraduate studying in the line of commerce. He always had a knack for writing but never went serious with it. That was until some time ago, after which he did.

He started writing as a hobby for he was always interested in creative writing. So he started his writing career with a poem and a novel simultaneously.

"A place to share my skills" Is what Prateek always envisions. And so the idea of creating a webiste popped up in his mind. Always getting top scores in language and literature, he honed his skills in all the subjects until his attention was diverting.

From the state of West Bengal, India and living with his family, he always focuses on necessities and does not waste any single moment on things that would not help him anyway. Since childhood he has always had a curiousity to know everything. If there's a book, he tried to read all the books. If there's a movie, he watched all the movies. If there's any talks on media platforms giving information on topics like science, he would watch all the talks.

In short, he witnessed many things. And being the most fond of english, he ultimately developed a desire of wanting to write.
